## Governance

- Founder/CEO: management accountability and resources.
- Management Representative: QMS authority and independence.
- Clinical Safety Officer: clinical risk and residual-risk acceptance.
- Regulatory lead: intended purpose, classification, conformity and ARTG.
- Engineering lead: lifecycle, configuration, cybersecurity and V&V.
- Data/model owner: dataset, model, prompt and drift controls.
- Independent reviewer: design reviews and evidence challenge.

One individual may hold multiple roles during the lean phase, but approval independence must be explicit for safety-critical decisions.

## Program controls

- No retrospective document may imply a review occurred when it did not.
- Existing repository evidence must be classified as accepted, remediated or excluded.
- Safety-critical approvals require named people, dates, versions and evidence hashes.
- Model, prompt, dataset and clinical-content changes enter formal change control.
- The product may not be described as certified, registered or approved before evidence exists.
