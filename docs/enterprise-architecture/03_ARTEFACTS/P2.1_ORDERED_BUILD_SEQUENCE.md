# P2.1 Ordered Build Sequence

**Decision:** M-D022  
**Constraint:** every increment is synthetic/staging-only and cannot open patient eligibility.

| Order | Increment | Exit evidence | Blocking input/gate |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Activate authoritative two-backend clinical evaluation | Live-mode provenance proven per backend; approved Claude and MedGemma fixtures; signed-rubric reports; replay gate produces an actual blocking result | Workflow/live-proof remediation, representative case manifest, MedGemma reachability, staging credentials, protected workflow, thresholds and positional test |
| 2 | Complete verified clinician identity | Successful authorised clinician login plus negative-path, role, revocation and audit evidence | IdP/protocol decision, credentials, privacy/security review |
| 3 | Monitored staging soak | Approved soak report covering stability, latency, alerts, WORM integrity, recovery and incidents | Increment 1 actual result, monitoring thresholds, pager owner |

## Stop conditions

Stop and fail closed if any of the following occurs:

- unsigned or wrong-version clinical rubric;
- a backend is missing from the evaluation result;
- critical under-triage occurs;
- sealed scoring data enters a model packet;
- a credential, identity, role or WORM control cannot be proven;
- a threshold would need to be weakened to pass;
- any work is proposed as patient-facing before regulatory and operator release gates are green.

## Parallel work permitted

Identity-provider procurement and monitoring setup may begin while the evaluation preflight is assembled. Neither may be represented as complete without its exit evidence, and neither authorises patient activity.
