## Advice requested

## Product boundaries for review

### A. Makoha Triage

Directly collects and analyses user health information, identifies presentations that may require urgent or emergency assessment, and provides a disposition to seek emergency care, obtain prompt professional assessment, or proceed to a defined professional-led pathway. Incorrect or absent disposition may delay time-critical treatment.

**Working classification:** Class III.

### B. Makoha Professional Assurance

Provides registered pharmacists and medical practitioners with traceable information and recommendations supporting history collection, red-flag identification, differential consideration, investigation, treatment/intervention selection, prescribing review, escalation and documentation. The professional decides and signs.

**Working classification:** Class IIb.

### C. Makoha Record and Workflow Services

Stores, retrieves, transfers and displays patient-authorised information, consent, provenance, audit records and administrative forms without therapeutic analysis.

**Working position:** separate non-device or lower-risk assessment.
