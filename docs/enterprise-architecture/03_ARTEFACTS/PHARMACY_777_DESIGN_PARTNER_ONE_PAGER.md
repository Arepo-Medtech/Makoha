# Makoha × Pharmacy 777

**Founder-approved discussion document — 21 July 2026**

## Non-Exclusive Consultation Assurance Design Partnership

### The opportunity

As prescribing-pharmacist services expand, pharmacy groups need consistent evidence that consultations follow approved eligibility, exclusion, escalation, communication and documentation requirements—without weakening pharmacist judgement or duplicating mandated clinical systems.

Makoha is developing a governance-first consultation-assurance platform for authorised health professionals. We propose a six-week, non-exclusive discovery and synthetic-evidence phase with Pharmacy 777.

### What we will do

- Map the current prescribing-pharmacist workflow and governance responsibilities.
- Identify material gaps and duplication against current clinical systems.
- Demonstrate the proposed Makoha assurance workflow using synthetic cases only.
- Tabletop safety, professional-accountability, privacy, security, integration and usability risks.
- Deliver a findings report and a governed next-stage evidence protocol—or recommend stopping.

### What we need

- One group sponsor.
- One clinical-governance/service leader.
- One Toowoomba prescribing pharmacist.
- One technology/workflow owner.
- Up to four structured workshops and factual review of the resulting workflow map.

No patient data, production credentials or clinical deployment are requested.

### Commercial terms

- Six weeks.
- AUD $0 Makoha fee for the defined discovery/synthetic phase.
- Each party bears its own internal costs.
- Entirely non-exclusive; no rollout commitment or automatic conversion.
- Either party may stop, including immediately for safety, privacy, security, professional or regulatory concerns.
- Any later evidence phase is separately scoped, approved and priced.

### Safeguards

- Pharmacists retain clinical judgement and professional accountability.
- Makoha is not represented as approved, clinically validated or available for patient care.
- No patient-identifiable information or model training on Pharmacy 777 data.
- Intellectual property and confidential information remain with their existing owner.
- Publicity, logo use and case studies require separate written approval.
- Exact Toowoomba ownership and contracting parties are `TBC` and must be confirmed before execution, data access or site activity.

### The decision after six weeks

Proceed only if Pharmacy 777 confirms a material problem, Makoha demonstrates a useful non-duplicative role, critical risks are bounded and an authorised sponsor approves a next step. Otherwise, revise or stop.

### Proposed first conversation

A 45-minute discussion with Pharmacy 777's clinical-governance and technology leaders to test fit, confirm participants and agree the discovery boundary.
