# Regulated-Venture Milestone-to-Capital Planner

**Skill ID:** S-M007  
**Status:** DRAFT — used once  
**Owner:** Founder / Finance / Regulatory / Quality

## Trigger

Use when a regulated venture needs a costed operating plan, capital requirement, runway model or funding thesis linked to evidence and release gates.

## Inputs

- Current cash, liabilities, burn and commitments.
- Founder capacity and compensation requirements.
- Product/regulatory posture and unresolved gates.
- Milestones, accountable owners and completion evidence.
- Hiring, supplier and adviser delivery model.
- Credible contracted or probability-weighted revenue.
- Funding preferences, cap table and control constraints.

## Procedure

1. Separate verified actuals, signed commitments and planning assumptions.
2. Define capital-constrained, base and accelerated outcomes before costing.
3. Build a monthly milestone spine with explicit safety, regulatory, quality, evidence and commercial gates.
4. Cost by accountable workstream, then add visible contingency and reserve.
5. Include only contracted or explicitly provisional revenue; never rely on unapproved patient/production revenue.
6. Calculate financing need from gross spend, credible revenue, reserve, liabilities, opening cash and committed funds.
7. Build an editable workbook with central assumptions, monthly phasing, cash roll-forward, scenarios, checks and sources.
8. Define bridge, principal round and next-round milestone logic where relevant.
9. Stress-test founder control, IP, data, exclusivity, strategic rights and dilution.
10. Set stop/narrow rules that prevent cash pressure weakening clinical or quality controls.
11. Record the selected planning case, unverified inputs, artefacts and phase-gate status.

## Required outputs

- 12-month operating plan.
- Low/base/high cost scenarios.
- Monthly cash and runway workbook.
- Funding thesis and use-of-funds logic.
- Founder-control and strategic-rights guardrails.
- Checks, sources, decision and gap updates.

## Guardrails

- Never present a zero placeholder as actual cash or no liabilities.
- Never convert a planning envelope into an approved raise, runway or valuation claim.
- Do not count unsigned revenue, grants or partner funding as cash.
- Patient activity follows release gates, not budget dates.
- Do not remove quality, clinical, regulatory or security work merely to force a preferred raise size.
- Do not recommend an instrument without current cap-table, legal and tax analysis.
- Protect background IP, data rights, non-exclusivity and clinical decision independence.

## Maturity rule

Promote from `DRAFT` after verified actuals replace placeholders, an independent finance/board review is completed and a second reforecast reproduces useful results.
